MENSTRUAL PHASE

Menstrual phase is the third stage of the menstrual cycle.
At this stage you get your perios, and this is bacause the egg from the previous cycle has not been ferterlized.
Since pregnancy has not taken place, levels of estrogen and progesterone drop.
Additionally, since the thickened lining of the uterus that is formed to support a pregnancy is no longer needed it sheds through your vagina together with blood and mucus.

SYMPTOMS

  • cramps
  • tender breasts
  • bloating
  • mood swings
  • irritability
  • headaches
  • tiredness
  • low back pain

NUTRITION

In this phase, Iron-rich foods which helps to transport oxygen throughout your body to avoid feeling exhausted. Proteins, and vitamins for energy and recovery are crucial. Foods that support blood replenishment and reduce fatigue are beneficial.
