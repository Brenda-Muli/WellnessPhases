OVULATION PHASE

Ovulation is the second phase of the cycle.
It is when your ovary releases a mature egg.The egg travels down the fallopian tube toward the uterus to be fertilized by sperm.

SYMPTOMS

During this phase likely symptoms include:

  • changes in Basal Body Temperature (BBT):
  • lower abdominal pain
  • changes in Cervical Mucus
  • increased Libido
  • breast Tenderness:
  • trouble sleeping
  • mood changes

NUTRITION
